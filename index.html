Departments

Information system

To build an excellent reputation at national, regional and international levels by providing a quality, effective and efficient computer science education to the students.
To produce quality computer scientists at bachelor, master and doctoral levels who could be leaders, researchers, educators, problem solvers, life-long-learners and entrepreneurs by involving stakeholders and applying principles of continual quality improvement to enhance its program and faculty.

Information Technology

Building an excellent reputation at national, regional and international levels by providing quality, effective and efficient IT education to the students based on acceptable standards and IT curriculum framework.
Producing graduates that can meet the local and regional IT job requirements and skills demand based on a standard competency based IT curriculum model in consideration of the local needs and futuristic view IT domains.

Software Engineering

Software Engineering Department will act to be the center of excellence for studies and research. The graduates of this department will be recognized as high skilled software developers, analytical and critical thinkers in the field of software engineering and they will contribute in the development and economy of the country by providing the needed software and services.
