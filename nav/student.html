Talent of Computer science Faculty

Najeebullah Karimi:

My name is Najeebullah Karimi. Born in the leap of month of 2004 while away from my nation of Afghanistan due to the mentioned circumstances, my family & I returned to the country when I was 5. Living in Kabul, Afghanistan, a year later in 2010, I embarked on my journey of formal education by enrolling in the Amar Baba Private High School. Learning the ABCs of everything for the next decade & two at the institution, the great school time finally came to its conclusion when I thankfully graduated summa cumma laude in 2021. Saying farewell to school, the next mission was landing a spot in my favorite university’s desired faculty which was none other than Kabul University’s Computer Science! It all started during childhood when I was amazed by the multitude of games I binge played. I wanted to know how they were made & make my own ones one day. That’s how my enthusiasm gradually started shifting towards the study of computers till I finally decided to call it my future profession & career. Specially targeting the Software Engineering major, Alhamdulillah, I was able to pass the 2022 national Kankor exam with flying colors, scoring 338.5 points & booking my ticket to the the best & oldest university in the nation! Being a sophomore of the class of 2026 at the time of writing, I hope the final two years of university will pack me with all the knowledge required to further pursue my studies in the field of SE & be able serve the whole world one day! From the multiple tracks available, I’m mostly leaning towards mobile development & artificial intelligence, hoping to master them in the future thereby playing an active role in solving the globe’s problems with the powers of digital computing.
