DEPARTMENT OF INFORMATION SYSTEMS

  • To build an excellent reputation at national, regional and international levels by providing a quality, effective and efficient computer science education to the students.
  • To produce quality computer scientists at bachelor, master and doctoral levels who could be leaders, researchers, educators, problem solvers, life-long-learners and entrepreneurs by involving stakeholders and applying principles of continual quality improvement to enhance its program and faculty.
  • Participate and promote inter-disciplinary research and innovative activities among professionals and contribute to the Afghanistan’s economy and self-sustainability.
  • To educate highly qualified students based on widely accepted educational practices for a successful professional career.
  • To provide a learning environment that can help students learn professional, research, leadership, problem-solving, life-long-learning and entrepreneurship skills.
  • To produce graduates with necessary professional skills that can propose, plan, design, build, develop, manage, maintain and secure technological solutions and infrastructures that can help Afghanistan in building a transparent, reliable, strong and self-sustainable economy and governance.
  • Ability to design, implement and evaluate a computer-based system or program that meets the needs of organizational stakeholders.
  • Ability to criticize interface front-end, system back-end, analyze data and apply technical solutions to computerized systems.
  • Ability to work collaboratively and communicate effectively with information system stakeholder’s /business partners, users, customers, third-party providers as well as system analysts, designers and developers.

DEPARTMENT OF INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY

  • Building an excellent reputation at national, regional and international levels by providing quality, effective and efficient IT education to the students based on acceptable standards and IT curriculum framework.
  • Producing graduates that can meet the local and regional IT job requirements and skills demand based on a standard competency based IT curriculum model in consideration of the local needs and futuristic view IT domains.
  • Being part of global research communities and actively contribute to the computer science knowledge and promote research and innovation among the Afghan computer scientists.
  • Graduate highly qualified students based on a widely accepted IT educational standards and curriculum framework (ACM/IEEE IT2017) and guidelines for a successful professional career considering the Afghanistan’s job market and stakeholder needs.
  • Provide a learning environment that can help students to learn professional, research, leadership, problem-solving, lifelong learning and entrepreneurship skills.
  • Produce graduates in the field of IT with necessary professional skills that can propose, plan, design, build, develop, manage, maintain and secure technological solutions and infrastructures that can help Afghanistan in building a transparent, reliable, strong, self-sustainable and affordable IT systems

This department’s objective is to train IT professionals in the field of Information and Communication Technology. The IT Department curriculum is also aligned and focused to achieve this objective. The IT professionals that will be graduated from this department can be employed at various organizations and they can work as system administrators, network administrator, network engineers, network specialists, network designers, security experts, IT managers, web developer and database administrators; students are also familiar with cybercrimes.

DEPARTMENT OF SOFTWARE ENGINEERING

Software Engineering Department will act to be the center of excellence for studies and research. The graduates of this department will be recognized as high skilled software developers, analytical and critical thinkers in the field of software engineering and they will contribute in the development and economy of the country by providing the needed software and services.

  • To provide high quality education for the graduate and undergraduate students of this department, enabling them to be successful leaders and innovators capable of meeting complex challenges.
  • To promote qualities of leadership professionalism, teamwork spirit, critical and analytical thinking, and cultural sensitivity among staff and students.
  • To ensure training of students so that they have sound moral and ethical values, thereby becoming a useful part of the society and thus contributing positively to the socio-economic growth of the country.
  • Ability to design, develop, test, maintain, reengineer and analyze different types of software systems.
  • Ability to manage a software project from writing proposal, planning and scheduling, risk management, monitoring, and reporting using modern project management tools such as, configuration management tools.
  • Ability to apply knowledge of science, mathematics, computer science principles and other relevant field of studies to solve complex computing problems.
